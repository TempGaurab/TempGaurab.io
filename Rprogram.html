Manchester City Games Analysis


I led a collaborative research with my team to collect and analyze data on Football/Soccer games from the English Premier league. This involved:

  1. Analyzed Premier League data spanning from 1993 to 2023, encompassing table positions and goals scored. The data was collected from a reputed source which I downloaded from Google Scholar.
  2. Utilized Pandas and Numpy to compute information on winners, runner-ups, trophies, and relegations.
  3. Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to produce visual graphs using matplotlib and seaborn, accessible at: link


Then we focused on one single team(Manchester City) and analyzed their games from 2018 to present. We did the following:

  1. Extracting information from the Google Football/Soccer Database using Beautiful Soup.
  2. Employing Pandas to clean the dataset thoroughly.
  3. Strategically sampling games to eliminate bias.
  4. Conducting a statistical analysis using the R programming language to explore the details of the dataset in depth.
  5. Developing a sophisticated predictive linear regression model as the culmination of our efforts.
  6. Fine-tuning the model through the strategic application of backward elimination techniques to ensure accurate forecasts of the total goals scored by Manchester City.
