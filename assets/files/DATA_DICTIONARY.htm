Variable Name Type(Range) Description
buildUpPlaySpeed Quantitative(20 to 76) Refers to the speed at which a team progresses the ball from their own half towards the opponent's goal during the build-up phase of play.
buildUpPlayDribbling Quantitative(0 to 77) The ability of players to advance the ball by dribbling past opponents during the build-up phase.
buildUpPlayPassing Quantitative(20 to 67) Involves the passing accuracy and effectiveness of a team when moving the ball forward from defense to attack.
buildUpPositioningClass Categorical(Free Form and Organized) Describes the tactical approach of a team during the build-up phase of their attacks."Free Form": Indicates that the team's positioning during build-up is fluid and less structured, allowing players more freedom to move. "Organised": Indicates that the team's positioning during build-up is structured and regimented, with players adhering to specific positional instructions.
chanceCreationPassing Quantitative (21 to 77) The quality and effectiveness of passing leading to goal-scoring opportunities.
chanceCreationCrossing Quantitative(20 to 80) Refers to the ability of players to deliver accurate crosses into the opposition's penalty area, creating goal-scoring chances.
chanceCreationShooting Quantitative(22 to 80) Indicates the proficiency of players in taking shots on goal to create scoring opportunities.
chancecreationPositioning Categorical(Free Form and Organized) Describes the tactical approach of a team during the creation of scoring chances."Free Form": Indicates that the team's positioning during chance creation is fluid and less structured, allowing players more freedom to move and create opportunities. "Organised": Indicates that the team's positioning during chance creation is structured and regimented, with players adhering to specific positional instructions to create scoring opportunities.
defencePressure Quantitative(23 to 72) The level of pressure applied by a team to regain possession or disrupt the opponent's build-up play.
defenceAggression Quantitative(30 to 72) Measures the aggressiveness and intensity of a team's defensive efforts, including challenges, interceptions, and tackles.
defenceTeamWidth Quantitative(34 to 70) Describes how wide or compact a team's defensive shape is when out of possession, influencing their ability to defend wide areas and prevent opponents from exploiting space.
defenceDefenderLineClass Categorical(Cover and Offside Trap) Describes the defensive strategy used by a team in terms of the positioning of their defenders."Offside Trap": Indicates that the team's defensive line employs an offside trap strategy, attempting to catch opposition players offside by moving the defensive line forward at opportune moments. "Cover": Indicates that the team's defensive line employs a cover strategy, focusing on maintaining a compact shape and providing support to each other to defend against opponent attacks.
Goals Scored Quantitative(36 to 121) The total number of goals a team has scored in a given period or competition.
Goals Conceded Quantitative(18 to 61) The total number of goals a team has allowed the opposition to score in a given period or competition.
Total Points Quantitative(38 to 102) The cumulative points earned by a team in a league or tournament, usually awarded for wins and draws, used to determine their ranking or position in the standings.
Champions League Categorical(Yes and No) Yes if the team qualified for champions league and no if it did-not.


Note: All quantitaive variables except for Goals Scored, Goals Conceded and Total Points must be between 0 and 100.
